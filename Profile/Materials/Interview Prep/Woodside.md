# Woodside — Interview Prep

## Company Overview

Woodside Energy Group Ltd is a major global energy company headquartered in Australia. Focuses on oil, natural gas, and LNG; one of Australia's largest independent energy producers.

Expanding U.S. presence along the Gulf Coast / Houston: LNG infrastructure, deepwater oil, lower-carbon transition investments.

Emphasizes: operational excellence, capital discipline, digital transformation, reducing emissions intensity.

**Purpose:** "Society's trusted energy partner."
**Mission:** Deliver affordable energy solutions and superior outcomes for stakeholders.
**Vision:** Provide reliable, lower-carbon energy; global leader in energy transition.

### Values
- **One team** — collaboration across functions and geographies
- **We care** — people, communities, environment
- **We innovate every day** — creativity, better solutions
- **Our results matter** — delivering performance
- **We build and maintain trust** — integrity and accountability

## Role: Digital, IT & Data Internship

Part of Woodside's USA Early Career Pathways initiative. Project-based, hands-on.

**Focus areas:** data analysis & visualization, systems optimization, BI/reporting, process automation, cybersecurity support, digital tools adoption, enterprise application support (SAP, cloud, analytics).

Work ties to: operational efficiency, cost optimization, performance tracking, risk mitigation.

Internship is a primary feeder into Woodside's graduate/early-career programs.

## What They Evaluate
- Strong problem-solving ability
- Technical competence (data, software, systems, analytics)
- Communication skills
- Teamwork
- Adaptability and initiative
- Professionalism and accountability
- Safety mindset, integrity, structured decision-making, risk awareness
